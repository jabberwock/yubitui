<objective>
Wire multi-key support, touch policy display and setting, and attestation display into the TUI. Detection layer learns to enumerate multiple YubiKeys via `ykman list --serials` and pass `--device <serial>` to all subsequent commands. The App struct evolves from a single yubikey_state to a Vec of states with a selected index. Dashboard shows which key is active and allows Tab-switching. Key management screen displays touch policies, allows setting touch policy per slot (with irreversibility warning for Fixed/CachedFixed), and offers attestation certificate viewing.

Purpose: This is the user-facing integration of the backend modules from Plan 02 plus the multi-key detection expansion. After this plan, power users can manage multiple keys, see touch/attestation status, and change touch policies.

Output: Working multi-key switcher, touch policy display and set, attestation popup in the TUI.
</objective>
